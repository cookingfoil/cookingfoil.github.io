I am a Ph.D. student at Interactive Media Lab and enthusiastic researcher in the field of Human-Computer Interaction.
My research direction is to apply mathematical models to measure and quantify human performance in the everyday pointing task with a computer mouse.
I am a lover of adventure and derive great pleasure from playing computer games, driving and talking with inspiring people.

[Research topic]
1) Human-Computer Interaction
2) Mathematical modeling to measure and quantify the human performance in pointing task
3) Analyzing the player's behavior related to aiming in FPS(First-Person Shooting) game
